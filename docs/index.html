SHORT FUZE

4 players. One phone. Pure random chaos.

Short Fuze is a fast, loud, pass-the-bomb party game for you and three friends — all on one phone. Pass the bomb before the fuse burns out. Whoever's holding it when it blows takes the L. The catch? Nothing is predictable.

How to play

  • 4 players, one phone — each person takes a corner of the screen.
  • When the bomb lands on your corner, tap fast to pass it on.
  • The bomb jumps to a random player every pass.
  • The fuse secretly runs 8, 13, 21, or 34 seconds — you never know how long you've got.
  • Tap out of turn and you get the over-tap penalty: the bomb snaps back to you, minus 2 seconds.
  • Don't be holding it at zero. BOOM.
