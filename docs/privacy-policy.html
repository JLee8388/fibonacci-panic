Privacy Policy — Short Fuse

Last updated: 2026

Short Fuse (“the app”) is a local, single-device party game. Your privacy is simple to explain because the app is built not to touch your data.

Data we collect

None. Short Fuse does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

Specifically, the app:

  • Does not require an account or sign-in.
  • Does not collect names, emails, contacts, location, or device identifiers.
  • Does not include analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs.
  • Does not make any network requests — it runs fully offline.
  • Does not use cookies or persistent tracking of any kind.

Permissions

The app may trigger the device’s haptic feedback (vibration) to enhance gameplay. This does not access or collect any data.

Children’s privacy

Because the app collects no data whatsoever, it collects no data from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised “Last updated” date.
